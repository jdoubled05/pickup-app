### Description (4000 char max)
**This is what users see in the App Store**

```
Find basketball courts and live pickup games near you.

Pickup helps you discover local basketball courts and see who's playing right now. Whether you're looking for a quick game or your new home court, we've got you covered.

FEATURES

• Live Activity - See how many players are checked in at nearby courts
• Court Finder - Browse hundreds of basketball courts in your area
• Real-time Updates - Get notified when players check in nearby
• Weather Info - Check conditions before you head out
• Court Photos - See what courts look like before you go
• Save Favorites - Bookmark your go-to courts
• Directions - Get directions to any court with one tap

PERFECT FOR

• Finding pickup games in your neighborhood
• Discovering new courts while traveling
• Checking if your local court is busy
• Connecting with other ballers

HOW IT WORKS

1. Open the app and see courts near you
2. Check the "Hot Now" filter for live games
3. Tap a court to see details and photos
4. Check in to let others know you're playing
5. Get directions and head to the court

WHY PICKUP?

Stop showing up to empty courts. With Pickup, you can see who's playing before you go. Check in when you arrive so others can find you. Build your local basketball community.

Download Pickup and never play alone again.
```
